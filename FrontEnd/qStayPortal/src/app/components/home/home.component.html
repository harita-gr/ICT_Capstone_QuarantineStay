Quarantine Stays

Quarantine Stays is an initiative by State Government of Kerala to provide quarantine facilities to incoming travellers to the state. Our mission is to provide affordable stay options with Government verified hygiene facilities and medical support.

Eligible travellers can also apply for fully-paid quarantine facililities funded by government.

Currently we provide facililities only to International and Interstate travellers near all airport locations in Kerala.
